About Me

About Me

Chan Hur recently received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Kyungpook National University. His research focuses on multimodal learning, particularly in text-video retrieval, compositional image-text retrieval, and large vision-language models (LLMs/LMMs). He is interested in bridging semantic gaps between modalities through generative information and representation learning.

Research Interests and Skills

  • Multimodal Learning: Vision-Language Pretraining, Text-Video Retrieval, Text-Guided Image Retrieval
  • Representation Learning: Cross-modal alignment, Generative Caption Utilization
  • Deep Learning Frameworks: PyTorch, TensorFlow
  • Language Skills: Korean (native), English (fluent), Japanese (intermediate)
